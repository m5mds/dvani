About Us

Experience shaped
since 2009.

Founded in 2009, Divani is a Saudi design and delivery firm specializing in Interior & Architectural Design, Luxury Fit-Out Solutions and Real Estate Development. We work across hospitality, residential, commercial and destination-led environments, carrying each project from a clear spatial idea through disciplined execution.

Why DIVANI

Every Detail.
Intentionally Designed.

Divani protects one design vision from concept through delivery. Our multidisciplinary approach combines design judgment, material knowledge, technical coordination and precise execution to create environments tailored to the client, the place and how each space will be used.

  1. Read the ambitionClarify the place, its purpose and the standard it must hold.
  2. Resolve the systemBring space, material and essential decisions into one coherent direction.
  3. Carry it forwardKeep the defining idea legible as the work moves from intention into reality.
  4. Finish with disciplineReview the whole environment, not isolated details.
