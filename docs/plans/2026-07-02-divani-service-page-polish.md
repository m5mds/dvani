# Divani Service Page Polish

Goal: make the services page feel more curated, sequential, and premium without adding more content.

## Audit Notes

- The services page currently relies on a simple grid of equal cards, which feels generic compared with the more refined hero, project, drawer, and client proof systems.
- The content itself is useful, but the visual structure does not communicate the operational flow from design through delivery.
- A luxury/service page should make the scope easy to scan while still feeling composed and distinguished.

## Design Direction

- Replace the card grid with a service atelier layout: one right-aligned narrative pane and a quieter service ledger.
- Keep all existing service text, but present it as a sequence of decisions rather than disconnected cards.
- Use subtle separators, gold numbering, and dark material depth instead of heavier card repetition.
- Keep the existing CTA band and dark identity.
