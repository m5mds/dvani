# Divani Why-Us Matrix Polish

Goal: make the "why us" page feel as refined and dark as the rest of the site while keeping the decision reasons clear.

## Audit Notes

- The current page uses a beige `paper-section` with four generic reason cards.
- After the services, clients, and certificates passes, this light card block feels disconnected from Divani's dark luxury identity.
- The content is useful, but it should read as a decision framework rather than a generic benefits grid.

## Design Direction

- Replace the beige reason grid with a dark decision matrix.
- Keep all four existing reasons and descriptions.
- Add a concise Arabic heading that explains why these reasons matter.
- Use line-based hierarchy, gold numbering, and subtle material depth instead of heavy repeated cards.
