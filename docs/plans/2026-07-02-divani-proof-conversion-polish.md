# Divani Proof And Conversion Polish

## Objective

Continue the perfect UI/UX push by tightening the proof and conversion pages without changing the approved dark, Apple-like multipage direction.

## Current Evidence

- Clients, certificates, quote, and contact pages render cleanly with no overflow or runtime errors.
- The fixed WhatsApp chip is useful on broad pages, but it visually competes with quote/contact content on desktop.
- Client and certificate proof sections have too much dead vertical air compared with the calmer, tighter homepage/project rhythm.

## Design Rules

- Do not redesign the page architecture.
- Preserve dark identity and clean image rules.
- Keep direct contact paths visible, but avoid floating controls over forms or proof content.
- Tighten proof pages with spacing and surface polish, not extra content.

## Implementation Plan

- Hide the floating WhatsApp chip on quote and contact pages at all viewport sizes.
- Add a compact proof-page spacing modifier for clients and certificates pages.
- Slightly refine logo/certificate surfaces so proof content feels intentional and easier to scan.
- Regenerate, audit, screenshot, document, commit, and push.
